## Purpose

Mine ideas from one YouTube channel that reviews / showcases SaaS or web apps. For every app the channel covers, decide:

1. Could a GWDNBM-shaped version exist as one of our portfolio launches?
2. Is the original app vulnerable on the GWDNBM axes (ads, upsells, email spam, engagement bait)?
3. What's the audience tier × demand category fit? (See [`market-analysis.md`](./market-analysis.md).)

## Audit method

For each app the channel covers:

1. Watch / skim the video — note app name, link, claimed job-to-be-done
2. Visit the app — note pricing, target audience, signup friction, ad/spam presence
3. Score against [`market-analysis.md`](./market-analysis.md) scoring framework
4. Verdict: BUILD-ALIKE / PASS / WATCH / NICHE-FORK

### Verdict legend

- **BUILD-ALIKE** — clear opportunity to ship a GWDNBM-shaped competitor; add to portfolio queue
- **NICHE-FORK** — generic version is saturated, but a niche fork (specific audience) is open
- **WATCH** — interesting but needs more validation before committing
- **PASS** — saturated / wrong shape / GWDNBM-incompatible / TAM too small
